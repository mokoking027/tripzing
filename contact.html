Frequently Asked

Quick answers to common questions

How can I book a trip with Tripzing? +

You can book through our website by filling out the contact form, calling us directly, or emailing us at bookings@tripzing.com. Our travel experts will curate a personalized itinerary based on your preferences and budget.

Can I customize my itinerary? +

Absolutely! Every itinerary we create is fully customizable. Whether you want to add extra days, swap activities, or include special experiences — just let us know and we'll tailor it perfectly for you.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI payments, and bank transfers. For international bookings, we also support PayPal and wire transfers. EMI options are available for select packages.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Free cancellation is available up to 30 days before departure. Between 15–30 days, a 25% fee applies. Within 15 days, a 50% fee applies. Travel insurance is recommended for added flexibility.

Do you offer group discounts? +

Yes! Groups of 5 or more travelers receive special discounts ranging from 10–20% depending on the destination. Contact us for a custom group quote.
