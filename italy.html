10 Days of Exploration

The Grand Tour

Days 1-3

The Eternal City: Rome

Hotel Artemide Roscioli Restaurant / Da Enzo al 29
  • Day 1: Arrival & check-in, Evening walk near Trevi Fountain, Relaxed dinner.
  • Day 2: Ancient Rome - Explore the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.
  • Day 3: Vatican City - St. Peter’s Basilica, Vatican Museums, and the Sistine Chapel.
Days 4-6

Renaissance Heart: Florence & Tuscany

Hotel Spadai Trattoria ZaZa / Osteria Vini e Vecchi Sapori
  • Day 4: Train to Florence, Explore Piazza del Duomo, Evening gelato.
  • Day 5: Florence Art & Culture - Uffizi Gallery, Florence Cathedral, Ponte Vecchio.
  • Day 6: Tuscany Day Trip - Pisa Leaning Tower, Tuscany countryside, Wine tasting.
Days 7-10

The Floating City: Venice

Hotel Antiche Figure Trattoria Al Gazzettino / Dal Moro’s Fresh Pasta
  • Day 7: Train to Venice, Scenic Gondola ride, Evening canals walk.
  • Day 8: Venice Exploration - St. Mark’s Basilica, Doge’s Palace, Rialto Bridge.
  • Day 9: Murano & Burano - Visit glass factories (Murano) and colorful houses (Burano).
  • Day 10: Souvenir shopping, Departure from Venice ✈️.
