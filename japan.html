Iconic Destinations

Explore the diverse beauty of Japan

Tokyo

A bustling metropolis combining neon-lit skyscrapers with historic temples.

The cultural heart of Japan, famous for classical Buddhist temples and gardens.

Osaka

Known for its modern architecture, nightlife and hearty street food.

Japan's northernmost island, famous for its volcanoes, natural hot springs, and ski areas.

Japan’s highest mountain and most iconic volcano, surrounded by beautiful lakes.
