The Journey

Your Travel Diary

Day 1 & 2

Mysore Royals & Transit

Morning to Afternoon

Arrive and dive into history with a visit to the grand Mysore Palace and the vast Mysore Zoo.

Evening

Stroll through Brindavan Gardens. On Day 2, take the scenic drive via Bandipur National Park towards the hills of Ooty.

Day 3 & 5

Ooty Exploration & Nature

Morning

Visit the vibrant Botanical Garden and Rose Garden. Later in the week, hike up to Doddabetta Peak for panoramic views.

Afternoon

Enjoy peaceful boating on Ooty Lake and Pykara Lake surrounded by tall eucalyptus trees.

Day 4

Coonoor Day Trip

Morning

Board the heritage Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train) winding through spectacular valleys and tunnels.

Afternoon

Disembark in Coonoor. Visit the breathtaking Dolphin's Nose viewpoint and walk through endless emerald tea estates.

Day 6 & 7

Leisure & Departure

All Day

Dedicate Day 6 to café hopping, eating homemade chocolates, and local shopping in Ooty town.

Departure

On Day 7, take a crisp morning walk before beginning your return journey home. 🌿

Need to Know

Travel Tips

⛅ Weather

Pleasant summers (15-20°C) and chilly winters (5-12°C). Always carry a light jacket.

🧳 Packing

Comfortable walking shoes for gardens, umbrellas for sudden mist/rain, and warm layers.

📅 Best Time

October to June is ideal. Avoid monsoons (July-August) due to heavy rainfall and leeches.
