Gangtok Escape

Where Clouds Meet the Mountains — Explore the soulful monasteries, azure lakes, and vibrant culture of Sikkim.

The Experience

5 NIGHTS & 6 DAYS OF HIMALAYAN SPLENDOR

Day 01

Arrival & City Vibes

  • Arrival at Bagdogra (IXB) and transfer to the misty hills of Gangtok.
  • Check-in at a premium boutique hotel with mountain views.
  • Evening stroll at MG Marg — the cleanest and most vibrant street in India.
  • Local dinner featuring authentic Sikkimese delicacies.

Day 02

High Altitude Wonders

  • Drive to the sacred Tsomgo Lake situated at 12,400 ft.
  • Visit the Baba Harbhajan Singh Memorial Mandir.
  • Optional visit to the historic Nathula Pass (Indo-China Border).
  • Evening at leisure to explore local cafes.

Day 03 & 04

Journey to the North

  • Transfer to Lachen — the gateway to North Sikkim.
  • Early morning visit to Gurudongmar Lake (17,800 ft), one of the highest lakes in the world.
  • Drive to Lachung through scenic waterfalls and apple orchards.
  • Overnight in cozy mountain lodges.

Day 05

Valley of Flowers

  • Explore the breathtaking Yumthang Valley (The Valley of Flowers).
  • Visit the hot springs known for their medicinal properties.
  • Option to visit Zero Point for snow activities.
  • Evening return to Gangtok.

Day 06

Sacred Spirits & Farewell

  • Visit the Rumtek Monastery — the largest in Sikkim.
  • Explore the Do Drul Chorten Stupa and Namgyal Institute of Tibetology.
  • Final souvenir shopping at the local market.
  • Departure for Bagdogra with memories for a lifetime.

Mountain Wisdom

ESSENTIALS FOR YOUR JOURNEY

Permits

Sikkim requires Inner Line Permits for certain areas. Keep 10 passport photos and ID copies ready.

Altitude

North Sikkim is high altitude. Consult a doctor for AMS medication and stay hydrated.

Clothing

Weather is unpredictable. Pack thermals, heavy woollens, and a windcheater regardless of the season.
