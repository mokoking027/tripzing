Sacred Destinations

Explore The Island

Ubud

The cultural heart of Bali, surrounded by lush rainforests, terraced rice paddies, and ancient temples.

Uluwatu

Breathtaking limestone cliffs, legendary surf breaks, and mesmerizing sunset temple dances.

Seminyak

Experience upscale tropical luxury with chic beach clubs, boutique shopping, and vibrant nightlife.

Nusa Penida

An untamed island paradise featuring the iconic dinosaur-shaped Kelingking Beach cliffs.

Tanah Lot

A sacred Hindu shrine beautifully perched on a rocky outcrop amidst crashing ocean waves.

7 Days of Balance

The Bali Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Bali

Neo Plus Kuta Legian
  • Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport.
  • Hotel check-in and refresh.
  • Leisure day to relax and acclimatize to the tropical breeze.
Day 2

Nusa Dua & Uluwatu

  • Explore the beautiful Nusa Dua region.
  • Enjoy Water Sports at Tanjung Benoa.
  • Visit the majestic Uluwatu Temple perched on a sea cliff.
Day 3

Nusa Penida Tour

  • Depart from Sanur Harbour for Nusa Penida.
  • Witness the iconic T-Rex shaped Kelingking Beach.
  • Enjoy snorkeling in crystal clear waters.
  • Return to the main island by evening.
Day 4

Ubud Culture & Nature

  • Experience traditional art at local galleries.
  • Swing over the jungle at Uma Pakel (Bali Swing).
  • Purify your spirit at Tirta Empul Water Temple.
  • Cool off at the spectacular Tegenungan Waterfall.
Day 5

Waterbom & Seminyak

Uppala Villa Seminyak
  • Thrilling day at Waterbom Bali waterpark.
  • Check-in to your private luxury villa.
  • Evening exploration of upscale Seminyak beach clubs.
Day 6

Leisure & Wellness

  • A free day to explore, relax, and rejuvenate.
  • Recommended: Balinese spa treatments or beach club lounging.
Day 7

Farewell Bali

  • Check-out and final souvenir shopping.
  • Departure from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
  • Farewell Bali, carrying home memories of peace.
